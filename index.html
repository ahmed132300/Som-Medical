About Us

Som-Medical Equipment Services and Repairing, founded by Mubarak and Elsayed Saber in 2025, is a dynamic company based in Hargeisa, Somaliland, dedicated to enhancing healthcare delivery in the region. Specializing in the supply of high-quality medical machines, equipment, and accessories. Som-Medical also offers comprehensive services, including the repair, installation, and maintenance of hospital machinery. With a strong commitment to supporting the healthcare sector, the company ensures reliable access to essential spare parts, minimizing downtime and ensuring the continued operation of medical equipment. Beyond its core services, Som-Medical actively engages in community-oriented initiatives, including donation drives, educational programs, and outreach campaigns aimed at empowering local healthcare institutions.
